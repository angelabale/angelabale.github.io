Website for the association Salon Culture & Jeux Mathématiques

In collaboration with a team of 5 students, I contributed to the development of a website for an annual event hosted by the Salon Culture & Jeux Mathématiques. The website facilitated teacher registrations, allowing their students to attend the event.

My specific contributions included :
  • organizing team tasks using Trello
  • designing and implementing the database
  • developing essential features such as login, registration, and account pages
I was then given the responsibility to independently finalize the project, ensuring its successful deployment into production.

I implemented advanced functionalities, including :
  • importing data from a .csv file to automatically generate time slots
  • integrating a reservation system
  • implementing a printing feature using fpdf, enabling the generation of PDFs that could be sent to participants via email
